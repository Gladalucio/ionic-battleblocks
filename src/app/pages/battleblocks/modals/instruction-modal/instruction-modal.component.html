-----BATTLESHIPS-----

Welcome to this simple game of ! Your goal is to place your ships on the playing field, play against the computer and win!

- - - Follow the steps below to get started: - - -

  1. Click on a ship in the bottom left.
  2. Click somewhere on your side of the playing field.
  3. Choose in what direction you want your ship to be positioned.
  4. Repeat the above steps until you have placed all five of your ships.

Once all your ships are placed, the "Start"-button will become active and you can start whooping the computer's ass playing your game!

If you decide you aren't happy with the way you put your things down, just click the "Reset'-button which appears when you have placed at least one ship.
